About Eventers



Eventers was founded by Sri Lankan based event coordinators with global experience in event management who specialize in creating innovative, sophisticated and cutting edge events. With over 14 years of experience our event planners have developed a rich knowledge and understanding of the event, art and hospitality worlds: resulting in our reputation for creating truly unforgettable moments and experiences.

With efficient turnaround times we have our very own in house creative design team, a production team, video wall equipment, video mapping equipment, all types lighting systems, sound systems and fabricators, we deliver the best in the market. Our creative team encompasses of global experiences, skills and knowledge of events to bring together result driven and overwhelming events to you finger tips.

What We Do

Event
Management

We provide an event management solution that is tailored to your needs, ensuring your event is planned to perfection. From the basics like finding the venue and negotiating with suppliers, to the more intricate details of the logistics, you and your event is in safe hands. Our in-house creative design teams are thought leaders, bursting with contemporary and unique ideas that will leave a lasting impression on your event and guests.

Our technical event production team have their finger on the pulse with the latest tech trends and are experienced in producing stimulating events with an incredible eye for detail. Our large warehouse is bursting with the very latest technical equipment which combined with our unique décor and furniture will ensure a flawless occasion and fabulous atmosphere. Our unforgettable parties, fun days, conferences, awards dinners and other corporate events combine the finest in design and execution, to produce perfection for you, our client. Explore our website to see our real life events, and remember there is a handy contact form at the bottom of every page, if you wish to get in touch.

Audio
Visual

Staging, lighting & AV are all imperative to any event you wish to hold. Lighting and AV are crucial in creating the correct tone and atmosphere for your event. As a corporate event company, we manage product launches, conferences, gala dinners and much more. We can provide a full range of event production services that will enhance the visual presentation and design of your event.

Our services include;
  • Bespoke light production
  • Set design
  • Video production
  • Live streaming
  • Stage build
  • Led Walls
  • Video projections
  • Audio systems
  • And any additional services

Our creative event planners understand how important the visual representation of your company is. We will endeavour to create a stunning production for your event.

Designing
Solutions

Our in house designers are here to take care of all your design needs; from special events to corporate identity or personal projects, we are here to help.

Every project is approached with an artistic passion because we love what we do. We develop ever-lasting relationships with our clients to help build a strong foundation on which creative thinking and heart felt design can add value to their businesses and invoke their vision.

We pride ourselves on providing the best service and crafting communication materials that bring together rich ideas and intelligent design. Our passion for design and art is what strives us to make every clients design needs stand out among the rest with our added unique and personal touches.

Marketing
Solutions

A comprehensive marketing and promotional plan is important to the success of any event. We believe marketing doesn’t have to always cost a lot of money but sometimes grass roots marketing and a strong PR plan are just as effective if not more so. Developing strong media partners and leveraging sponsors existing media buys can really help stretch your marketing budget.

Not only can we help you market your event put we can create the look and feel of it with our outstanding design team to help make your marketing materials standout! We can also handle the designing and ordering of your promotional items like t-shirts, hats and cowbells. We are a full service event management company.

Email Blasts
Develop creative emails sent to potential participants to encourage registration for the event.

Social Media Marketing
Create detailed schedule for posts on all social media outlets, which include: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn and Pinterest.

Advertisement Buying
Research the most effective outlets to buy advertising space for the event, as well as creating the unique ad.

Collateral Design
We work with a graphic designer to produce effective advertising materials, including postcards and posters.

Media Relations
Produce press releases to send to local media and entice them to cover the event.
